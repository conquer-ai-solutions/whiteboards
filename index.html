04 · The Agentic Shift
This isn't about better tools. It's about a different model of work.
Most businesses have deployed AI as a feature. We build it as a layer — one that thinks, acts, and coordinates across your systems.
Before
AI as a tool you use
🖥People ask the AI a question. AI responds. Work continues manually.
🔄Each task requires a human trigger. Nothing happens automatically.
🗂AI operates in isolation — it doesn't know what's in your CRM, inbox, or calendar.
⚠️High manual overhead. Benefits don't compound. ROI is modest.
📦Off-the-shelf. Everyone has it. No differentiation.
→
After
AI as a system that works
⚡Agents monitor, decide, and act without waiting to be asked.
🔗Integrated across your systems — CRM, email, calendar, documents, data.
🎯Surfaces the right information to the right person at the right moment.
📈Benefits compound. Each workflow automated frees capacity for the next layer.
🔒Built for your business. Your data. Your IP. Your competitive edge.

06 · Discovery

Questions we want to explore
12 questions
Process & Volume
›
What does a typical week look like for your highest-performing people? Where does their time actually go?
›
Which tasks are repeated most often — and which ones take the longest relative to the value they create?
›
Where do things fall through the cracks? What's the most common preventable failure in your team's work?
Systems & Data
›
What are the core platforms your people live in daily? Where does data currently sit and how does it move?
›
Are there systems that don't talk to each other — where people are manually bridging the gap?
›
What's your current position on data governance and cloud infrastructure?
Ambition & Constraints
›
If you could remove one operational constraint in the next 12 months, what would it be?
›
What's driven urgency on AI now — and who in the business is the strongest advocate?
