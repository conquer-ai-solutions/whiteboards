05 · Discovery
Five questions. That's all we need.
Not an intake form. Each question is designed to unlock something the previous conversation missed.
The five questions
Take as long as you need on each one.
01
If your highest performer left tomorrow, what would fall apart first?
Surfaces institutional knowledge risk, undocumented process, and the single point of failure no one talks about.
02
What's the last thing your team was doing at 6pm on a Friday that they shouldn't have been?
Cuts through the idealised version. Gets to the real high-frequency, high-friction work that's hiding in plain sight.
03
Where do things fall through the cracks — and who catches them?
Reveals handoff failures, informal QA processes, and the people quietly holding operations together.
04
What decision do you make every week that you don't have good enough data for?
Opens the data quality conversation without sounding like a BI pitch. Gets to the real information gap and its downstream cost.
05
What would have to be true for you to say, six months from now, that this was the best decision you made this year?
Sets the success criteria in their words. Creates shared accountability. Ends the session with a concrete definition of done.
